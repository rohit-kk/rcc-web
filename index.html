About Us

Authorized Learning Centre
Since 2002

RCC Institute of Computer Education is a premier, government-authorized computer training center located near Govt. Model School, Preet Nagar, Barara, Sharaqpur, Haryana 133201. Since our establishment in 2002, we have been committed to delivering quality, job-oriented education that empowers students to thrive in today's technology-driven world.

As an Authorized Learning Centre from Haryana State Corporation Ltd., our certifications carry government recognition — giving our students a genuine competitive edge in the job market. Our mission is to bridge the gap between education and employment by providing practical, industry-relevant training across computers, programming, accounting, design, and digital skills.

Our courses are designed and taught by experienced faculty members who bring real-world expertise into the classroom. With modern lab infrastructure, a hands-on teaching approach, and personalized attention for every student — from first-time learners to aspiring professionals — we prepare students not just for exams, but for successful careers.
